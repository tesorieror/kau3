The Goal of the Unit

The goal of the Unit is to improve the quality of KAU higher education. And draw the action plans to improve. These action plans are given three times per year, to and not just at the end of year. By this KAU can during the current year, view its weakness and work on it to improve before to continue in its growing path to reach excellence.

Unit Responsibilities

  • Collection and analysis of KAU data and evaluate its situation in the current higher education evaluation systems.
  • Research and planning of KAU higher education evaluation system.
  • Establishment of a talent pool and database for higher education evaluation.
  • Research on our own benchmarks for higher education evaluation.
  • Organize various evaluations and assessments.
  • Assist KAU in applying for international Higher Education Ranking and certification.
